Brotherhood

I'll start off by stating that I recommend just skipping the first 2 episodes entirely. At least for your 1st viewing. Trust me, you're not missing much. A lot of it is information that gets repeated in the 3rd episode, and a bunch of the foreshadowing in episode 1 went completely over my head the first time watching it.

It wasn't until rewatching the series did I realize, "OH, that's why you're like this". Most of it just doesn't make ANY sense at all without knowledge of one of the two series or the manga going in.

So my recommendation for first time viewers is to just skip the first 2 episodes.
