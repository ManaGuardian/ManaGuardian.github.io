An Essay on Death

A one page essay I wrote in the character of Necromancer about the ethics of his craft.
It was part of a small scale writing competition, for a group that will remain nameless. I wanted to show off his character, but also present an alternative view of Necromancy.
Some of it hasn't aged as well, or just won't make much sense if you're not familiar with the larger setting he's a part of.
